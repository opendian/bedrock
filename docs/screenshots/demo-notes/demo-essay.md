# On reading slowly

We confuse speed of reading with depth of reading. The two are not the same. A page read in two minutes, with full attention, leaves more behind than ten pages skimmed at five times the pace.

## The pattern

The trick is not to read more. The trick is to read with consequence — to let one sentence change what the next sentence means.

## In practice

Pick one short text. Set a timer for fifteen minutes. Read for the whole timer. When the timer ends, close the book and write down what you remember without checking. Whatever you can recall is what was actually read. The rest was eye motion.

## The closer

A small act, repeated, beats a grand plan abandoned.
