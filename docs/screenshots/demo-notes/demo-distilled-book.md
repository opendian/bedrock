# Show Your Work — demo distilled book note

A small example of the "distilled book" pattern this plugin reads well: short, structured, designed for audio.

## The gist

Most creative work goes unseen because the maker never shares the process. Sharing daily — drafts, sketches, dead ends — turns the work into a cabinet of curiosities that earns its own audience.

## Three principles

1. **Think process, not product.** The final thing is the tip of an iceberg. Share the iceberg.
2. **Tell good stories.** Not "I made a thing." Try "I was stuck on X for weeks, then noticed Y."
3. **Stick around.** Most people quit just before it starts working. Compounding is real and slow.

## The closer

Show your process, not your conclusions. The work is the cabinet.
